Our Profile

Bridging Design & Durability

CASA E VOCE DESIGN LTDA is a premier construction firm located in João Pessoa, Paraíba. Led by Gersica Thais Borges Do Rego Barros, we specialize in nonresidential and industrial building projects that require exacting standards.

Our name—"Casa e Voce Design"—reflects our core philosophy: balancing structural functionality with a personalized design approach. We understand that even industrial and commercial buildings must resonate with the people who use them.

From large-scale industrial complexes to commercial renovations, we bring a dedicated team of engineers, architects, and builders to every site, ensuring projects are delivered on time, within budget, and built to last.
